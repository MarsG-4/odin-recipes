Chocolate-Chip Cookies

One of the best cookie recipes out there!

A must try recipe if you love chocolate-chip cookies (especially if you like them chewy)

INGREDIENTS

Makes 30 to 35 cookies

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 8 ounces butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chopped bittersweet chocolate (chunks and shavings)
  • 2 cups chopped toasted walnuts (optional)

PREPARATION

  1. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper or Silpat. Sift together the flour, baking soda and salt.
  2. In a mixer fitted with a paddle, cream the butter and sugars until fluffy, 3 minutes. Add the eggs, one at a time, then the vanilla. Add the flour mixture all at once and blend until a dough forms. Fold in the chocolate and walnuts. Chill the dough for at least an hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 325. Roll 2 1/2-tablespoon lumps of dough into balls, then place on the baking sheet and flatten to 1/2-inch-thick disks spaced 2 inches apart. Chill the dough between batches. Bake until the edges are golden brown, 14 to 16 minutes. Let cool slightly on the baking sheet, then transfer to a baking rack.
